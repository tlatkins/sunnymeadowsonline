Welcome to Sunny Meadows!

Sunny Meadows is a sub-division located about 12 miles south of Birmingham, Alabama in unincorporated Shelby County. It is conveniently located between Valleydale Rd. and Cahaba Valley Rd. It is approximately 5 miles from U.S. Highway 280. The neighborhood is part of the Oak Mountain school district.

For questions about the neighborhood or the homeowner's association, please contact Bill Hart, president of the Sunny Meadows HOA, at (205) 862ignore this-0560.

For the latest news and updates, if you are a resident of Sunny Meadows, you can find us on Nextdoor.
